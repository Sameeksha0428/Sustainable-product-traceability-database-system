Our Story

Preserving Heritage Through Technology

EcoKatha is a project built to preserve and celebrate the rich heritage of handmade products. It traces the journey of every item from its origin — the hands of skilled local artisans — to its final destination with customers. EcoKatha aims to bring greater transparency to the craft industry, promote authentic storytelling behind each creation, and bridge the gap between traditional craftsmanship and a modern, conscious audience. Through this platform, we hope to not only support artisans but also create a deeper connection between people and the timeless art they cherish.

For development, we used MySQL to manage and organize the database, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to design a responsive and engaging frontend, and PHP to build a strong and functional backend. EcoKatha is not just a platform — it is a step towards supporting local talent, celebrating timeless art, and creating meaningful connections through technology.
